Role: Global Transformation Lead

Timeline: 6 months

Impact: 30% revenue increase

Challenge: British Airways required a unified digital maintenance solution across its global network to improve efficiency, transparency, and revenue management.

Solution: Andrew led the rollout of an MRO ERP across all stations, designing comprehensive training for over 500 staff, coordinating with IT, engineering, and operations, and implementing a robust change management framework.

Technologies: MRO-PRO, Power BI, SharePoint, Power Automate

Results: 300+ users onboarded, 30% revenue increase, >90% adoption after 6 months.

Key Learnings: Global rollouts require meticulous planning, stakeholder alignment, and continuous feedback loops. Structured training and clear benefit communication drive adoption and sustained usage.

Role: Architect & Developer
