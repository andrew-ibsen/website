## MSc Thesis: Tokenizing Aircraft Assets on a Distributed Ledger

**Institution:** City, University of London  
**Abstract:**  
Andrew's thesis investigates the feasibility of tokenizing aircraft assets using blockchain technology. The research validates accuracy improvements and cycle‑time reductions in asset tracking, offering a proof‑of‑concept for distributed ledger applications in aviation asset management.

**Key Takeaways:**
- Distributed ledgers can enhance transparency and reduce discrepancies in asset histories
- Tokenization enables granular ownership and lifecycle management of aircraft components
- Compliance and audit trails improve with tamper‑proof records

**Read Time:** 8 minutes
