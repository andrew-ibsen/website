About

Andrew Ibsen is an aviation operations leader with dual expertise in operational delivery and transformation portfolio management. Based in New York, he currently oversees British Airways’ largest overseas line station at JFK, managing 18–20 daily long-haul flights, leading a team of 35+ staff, and delivering $3.5M in annual third-party revenue. Andrew combines licensed engineering credentials with a master’s in aviation management and a focus on digital innovation, sustainability, and commercial outcomes.
