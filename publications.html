MSc Thesis: Tokenizing Aircraft Assets on a Distributed Ledger

Read Time: 8 minutes

Andrew's thesis investigates the feasibility of tokenizing aircraft assets using blockchain technology. The research validates accuracy improvements and cycle‑time reductions in asset tracking, offering a proof‑of‑concept for distributed ledger applications in aviation asset management. Key takeaways include enhanced transparency, granular ownership management, and improved compliance through tamper‑proof records.
